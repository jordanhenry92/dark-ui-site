Mission Statement:

When Dreams Become Memes was founded on a casual and friendly environment. Our goal is not only to get Cutting Edge every tier, but to have fun doing so with the people we enjoy raiding with.


Raid Times:

7 p.m. - 10 p.m. CST (server time) Tuesdays and Thursdays Progression Raid
7 p.m. - 10 p.m. CST (server time) Wednesdays (optional farm raid)

Our Commitment To You:

We value all our members and are committed to your success. We prefer to build our members rather than replace them. If the officer team notices you are struggling (with mechanics, output, attendance, timeliness, etc.) we will have a private conversation with you about what you need to improve on and offer our help to get you there. Have an open mind, accept constructive criticism, and execute. Do those things and you will always have a spot on our team.
