Officers

Officers consist of established, trusted guild members who will weigh in on all major guild decisions. officers show leadership with consistent attendance, postive attitudes, and by consistently demonstrating their willingness to help the guild succeed at any cost.

Champions

This rank is intended for the most dedicated and successful members of the raid team who have gone well above and beyond reasonable raider expectations and set a consistently positive example in every aspect. In recognition of their nearly perfect attendance and consistently exceptional performance in raids, Champions will be given the highest priority on raid invites, guild repairs, and flasks.

Raider

This is the standard for a successful raider; This is the bar. If any person has earned the raider rank they have proved that they are in good standing and have demonstrated they are an asset to this guild and are a welcome member to the raid group.

Reserve Raider

This person is not currently meeting the minimum standards to be considered a raider but has demonstrated they want to improve and has made an effort to do so. People assigned this rank are encouraged to seek the help of officers and raiders to identify and fix their specific issues to quickly resolve them.

Casual

The casual rank is reserved for members that are not interested in mythic raiding. They may be friends, family, former mythic raiders or random people that want to be a part our amazing atmosphere by hanging out with other Memers, running mythic+, participating in non-mythic runs, PvPing, and so on. All are welcome here! The more the merrier.

Trial

Trials are new members to the dream team. Please remember to welcome them and make them feel like they have found the best guild in WoW! The trial period will last 4 to 6 raids at the discretion of the officers. At the end of the trial period officers will use the raider guidelines to decide how to proceed. If trials are not a good fit for the group only due to raid performance reasons, they will be offered to stay on as casual members, otherwise they will be removed from the guild.
